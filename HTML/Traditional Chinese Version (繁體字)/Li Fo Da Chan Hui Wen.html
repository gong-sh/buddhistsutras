rú如 shì是 děng等 yī一 qiè切 shì世 jiè界  ， zhū諸 fó佛 shì世 zūn尊  ， cháng常 zhù住 zài在 shì世  。 shì是 zhū諸 shì世 zūn尊  ， dāng當 cí慈 niàn念 wǒ我  : ruò若 wǒ我 cǐ此 shēng生  ， ruò若 wǒ我 qián前 shēng生  ， cóng從 wú無 shǐ始 shēng生 sǐ死 yǐ以 lái來  ， suǒ所 zuò作 zhòng眾 zuì罪  ， ruò若 zì自 zuò作  ， ruò若 jiāo教 tā他 zuò作  ， jiàn見 zuò作 suí隨 xǐ喜  ; ruò若 tǎ塔  ， ruò若 sēng僧  ， ruò若 sì四 fāng方 sēng僧 wù物  ， ruò若 zì自 qǔ取  ， ruò若 jiāo教 tā他 qǔ取  ， jiàn見 qǔ取 suí隨 xǐ喜  ; wǔ五 wú無 jiān間 zuì罪  ， ruò若 zì自 zuò作  ， ruò若 jiāo教 tā他 zuò作  ， jiàn見 zuò作 suí隨 xǐ喜  ; shí十 bù不 shàn善 dào道  ， ruò若 zì自 zuò作  ， ruò若 jiāo教 tā他 zuò作  ， jiàn見 zuò作 suí隨 xǐ喜  ; suǒ所 zuò作 zuì罪 zhàng障  ， huò或 yǒu有 fù覆 cáng藏  ， huò或 bù不 fù覆 cáng藏  ， yīng應 duò墮 dì地 yù獄  、 è餓 guǐ鬼  、 chù畜 shēng生  ， zhū諸 yú餘 è惡 qù趣  、 biān邊 dì地 xià下 jiàn賤  ， jí及 miè篾 lì戾 chē車  ， rú如 shì是 děng等 chù處  ， suǒ所 zuò作 zuì罪 zhàng障  ， jīn今 jiē皆 chàn懺 huǐ悔  。  (一拜) 

jīn今 zhū諸 fó佛 shì世 zūn尊  ， dāng當 zhèng證 zhī知 wǒ我  ， dāng當 yì憶 niàn念 wǒ我  。 wǒ我 fù復 yú於 zhū諸 fó佛 shì世 zūn尊 qián前  ， zuò作 rú如 shì是 yán言  : ruò若 wǒ我 cǐ此 shēng生  ， ruò若 wǒ我 yú餘 shēng生  ， céng曾 xíng行 bù布 shī施  ， huò或 shǒu守 jìng淨 jiè戒  ， nǎi乃 zhì至 shī施 yù與 chù畜 shēng生  ， yī一 tuán摶 zhī之 shí食  ; huò或 xiū修 jìng淨 xíng行  ， suǒ所 yǒu有 shàn善 gēn根  ; chéng成 jiù就 zhòng眾 shēng生  ， suǒ所 yǒu有 shàn善 gēn根  ; xīu修 xíng行 pú菩 tí提  ， suǒ所 yǒu有 shàn善 gēn根  ; jí及 wú無 shàng上 zhì智  ， suǒ所 yǒu有 shàn善 gēn根  。 yī一 qiè切 hé合 jí集  ， jiào校 jì計 chóu籌 liàng量  ， jiē皆 xī悉 huí迴 xiàng向  ， ā阿 nòu耨 duō多 luó羅 sān三 miǎo藐 sān三 pú菩 tí提  。 rú如 guò過 qù去  、 wèi未 lái來  、 xiàn現 zài在 zhū諸 fó佛  ， suǒ所 zuò作 huí迴 xiàng向  ， wǒ我 yì亦 rú如 shì是 huí迴 xiàng向  。 zhòng眾 zuì罪 jiē皆 chàn懺 huǐ悔  ， zhū諸 fú福 jìn盡 suí隨 xǐ喜  ; jí及 qǐng請 fó佛 gōng功 dé德  ， yuàn願 chéng成 wú無 shàng上 zhì智  。 qù去  、 lái來  、 xiàn現 zài在 fó佛  ， yú於 zhòng眾 shēng生 zuì最 shèng勝  。 wú無 liàng量 gōng功 dé德 hǎi海  ， wǒ我 jīn今 guī皈 mìng命 lǐ禮  。  (一拜) 

suǒ所 yǒu有 shí十 fāng方 shì世 jiè界 zhōng中  ， sān三 shì世 yī一 qiè切 rén人 shī師 zǐ子  。 wǒ我 yǐ以 qīng清 jìng淨 shēn身 yǔ語 yì意  ， yī一 qiè切 biàn徧 lǐ禮 jìn盡 wú無 yú餘  。 pǔ普 xián賢 xíng行 yuan願 wē威 shén神 lì力  ， pǔ普 xiàn現 yī一 qiè切 rú如 lái來 qián前  。 yī一 shēn身 fù復 xiàn現 chà刹 chén塵 shēn身  ， yī一 yī一 biàn徧 lǐ禮 chà刹 chén塵 fó佛  。  (一拜) 

yú於 yī一 chén塵 zhōng中 chén塵 shù數 fó佛  ， gè各 chù處 pú菩 sà薩 zhòng眾 huì會 zhōng中  。 wú無 jìn盡 fǎ法 jiè界 chén塵 yì亦 rán然  ， shēn深 xìn信 zhū諸 fó佛 jiē皆 chōng充 mǎn滿  。 gè各 yǐ以 yī一 qiè切 yīn音 shēng聲 hǎi海  ， pǔ普 chū出 wú無 jìn盡 miào妙 yán言 cí辭  。 jìn盡 yú於 wèi未 lái來 yī一 qiè切 jié劫  ， zàn讚 fó佛 shèn甚 shēn深 gōng功 dé德 hǎi海  。  (一拜) 

yǐ以 zhū諸 zuì最 shèng勝 miào妙 huá華 màn鬘  ， jì伎 yuè樂 tú塗 xiāng香 jí及 sǎn傘 gài蓋  。 rú如 shì是 zuì最 shèng勝 zhuāng莊 yán嚴 jù具  ， wǒ我 yǐ以 gòng供 yǎng養 zhū諸 rú如 lái來  。 zuì最 shèng勝 yī衣 fú服 zuì最 shèng勝 xiāng香  ， mò末 xiāng香 shāo燒 xiāng香 yú與 dēng燈 zhú燭  。 yī一 yī一 jiē皆 rú如 miào妙 gāo高 jù聚  ， wǒ我 xī悉 gòng供 yǎng養 zhū諸 rú如 lái來  。 wǒ我 yǐ以 guǎng廣 dà大 shèng勝 jiě解 xīn心  ， shēn深 xìn信 yī一 qiè切 sān三 shì世 fó佛  。 xī悉 yǐ以 pǔ普 xián賢 xíng行 yuàn願 lì力  ， pǔ普 biàn徧 gòng供 yǎng養 zhū諸 rú如 lái來  。  (一拜) 

wǒ我 xī昔 suǒ所 zào造 zhū諸 è惡 yè業  ， jiē皆 yóu由 wú無 shǐ始 tān貪 chēn瞋 chī癡  。 cóng從 shēn身 yǔ語 yì意 zhī之 suǒ所 shēng生  ， yī一 qiè切 wǒ我 jīn今 jiē皆 chàn懺 huǐ悔  。  (一拜) 

shí十 fāng方 yī一 qiè切 zhū諸 zhòng眾 shēng生  ， èr二 chéng乘 yǒu有 xué學 jí及 wú無 xué學  。 yī一 qiè切 rú如 lái來 yǔ與 pú菩 sà薩  ， suǒ所 yǒu有 gōng功 dé德 jiē皆 suí隨 xǐ喜  。  (一拜) 

shí十 fāng方 suǒ所 yǒu有 shì世 jiān間 dēng燈  ， zuì最 chū初 chéng成 jiù就 pú菩 tí提 zhě者  。 wǒ我 jīn今 yī一 qiè切 jiē皆 quàn勸 qǐng請  ， zhuàn轉 yú於 wú無 shàng上 miào妙 fǎ法 lún輪  。  (一拜) 

zhū諸 fó佛 ruò若 yù欲 shì示 niè涅 pán槃  ， wǒ我 xī悉 zhì至 chéng誠 ěr而 quàn勸 qǐng請  。 wéi唯 yuàn願 jiǔ久 zhù住 chà刹 chén塵 jié劫  ， lì利 lè樂 yī一 qiè切 zhū諸 zhòng眾 shēng生  。  (一拜) 

suǒ所 yǒu有 lǐ禮 zàn讚 gòng供 yǎng養 fú福  ， qǐng請 fó佛 zhù住 shì世 zhuàn轉 fǎ法 lún輪  。 suí隨 xǐ喜 chàn懺 huǐ悔 zhū諸 shàn善 gēn根  ， huí迴 xiàng向 zhòng眾 shēng生 jí及 fó佛 dào道  。  (一拜) 

yuàn願 jiāng將 yǐ以 cǐ此 shèng勝 gōng功 dé德  ， huí迴 xiàng向 wú無 shàng上 zhēn真 fǎ法 jiè界  。 xìng性 xiàng相 fó佛 fǎ法 jí及 sēng僧 qié伽  ， èr二 dì諦 róng融 tōng通 sān三 mèi昧 yìn印  。 rú如 shì是 wú無 liàng量 gōng功 dé德 hǎi海  ， wǒ我 jīn今 jiē皆 xī悉 jìn盡 huí迴 xiàng向  。 suǒ所 yǒu有 zhòng眾 shēng生 shēn身 kǒu口 yì意  ， jiàn見 huò惑 tán彈 bàng謗 wǒ我 fǎ法 děng等  。 rú如 shì是 yī一 qiè切 zhù諸 yè業 zhàng障  ， xī悉 jiē皆 xiāo消 miè滅 jìn盡 wú無 yú餘  。 niàn念 niàn念 zhì智 zhōu周 yú於 fǎ法 jiè界  ， guǎng廣 dù度 zhòng眾 shēng生 jiē皆 bú不 tuì退  。 nǎi乃 zhì至 xū虛 kōng空 shì世 jiè界 jìn盡  ， zhòng眾 shēng生 jí及 yè業 fán煩 nǎo惱 jìn盡  。 rú如 shì是 sì四 fǎ法 guǎng廣 wú無 biān邊  ， yuàn願 jīn今 huí迴 xiàng向 yì亦 rú如 shì是  。  (一拜) 

ná南 mó無 dà大 xíng行 pǔ普 xián賢 pú菩 sà薩  。  （三稱畢起立）
